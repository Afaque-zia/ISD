We Strive For

ISD is based on the vision of a democratic, secular, and peaceful society. A society based on the mutual respect of rights of each individual and of human development.

We Strive For

ISD is based on the vision of a democratic, secular, and peaceful society.

A society based on the mutual respect of rights of each individual and of human development.

We belive in

Accepting identities and differences, empathizing with the socio-political and cultural aspirations of various social groups and opening dialogue without questioning intentions.

Our Vision

Unity in Diversity with Mutual Respect

To build a social structure based on peace, harmony and democratic values while accepting social and cultural diversity and establishing dialogue with mutual respect.

Our Mission

Strengthening Democracy Through Shared Heritage

To identify, understand and collect, different forms of Composite Heritage at the community level, through research and dialogues;

Mainstreaming of the concept of Composite Heritage through different mediums for the sake

To encourage the youth, like-minded groups and different peace networks at the South Asia Level, to contribute to peace and harmony by enhancing their knowledge and social analysis.

Our Belief

Belief in Dialogue, Dignity, and Democracy

ISD believes in establishing a dialogue with different social groups and connects with them with full respect towards their social and cultural aspirations and without doubting their intentions. ISD dreams of building a secular and democratic society based on human development and dignity of human rights.
